About Alora

Alora is dedicated to finding solutions to problems concerning dry cleaning and laundry service companies and their customers. We have created a product that alleviates the inconvenience of leaving your house to access laundry and dry cleaning services to a task that can be done from the comfort of your home.

Alora allows you to:

Schedule pick and delivery for your laundry and dry cleaning needs with our partner companies.
Track your orders.
View information about your orders.
Get laundry and dry cleaning tasks done without leaving your home

Contact us to become part of the Alora team. We aim to give your Laundry and/or Dry cleaning company the opportunity to expand and grow like never before. As a partner company of laundry, we provide a platform that allows you to expand your reach to your customers. We also allow you to manage your orders and pick up, and delivery needs.
