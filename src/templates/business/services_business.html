Welcome to Services

View the services your company offers.

Click the "Edit Services" button at the top of the page or scroll down to the add or update your services.

To add a new service:

  1. In the section labeled "Add new services":
  2. In the field labeled "Name" type the name of the new service you would like to add.
  3. Click the drop down labeled "Store" to select the name of your store.
  4. Click the "Create" button to add the new service.

To add an item to a service:

  1. In the section labeled "Add new item":
  2. In the field labeled "Name" type the name of the new item you would like to add to a service.
  3. Click the drop down labeled "Services" to select the service you would like to add the item to.
  4. In the field labeled "Price" type the price of the item.
  5. Click the "Add" button to add the new item.
